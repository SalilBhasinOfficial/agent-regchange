# POL-RMC-004 — Risk Management Committee of the Board: Charter
*Version 5.0. Effective 2025-01-01. Owner: Company Secretary. Approving Authority: Board of Directors.*

## 1. Constitution
The Risk Management Committee of the Board ("RMC") shall comprise not less
than three directors, of whom at least one shall be an independent director.
The Chairperson of the RMC shall be an independent director.

## 2. Frequency of Meetings
The RMC shall meet at least once every calendar quarter. Additional meetings
may be convened by the Chairperson.

## 3. Standing Agenda Items
At each meeting the RMC shall consider:
(i)   Capital adequacy ratios and buffer position.
(ii)  Credit concentration — single borrower, group borrower, industry, and
      geography.
(iii) Liquidity coverage, NSFR and intraday liquidity.
(iv)  Operational risk events and key risk indicators.
(v)   Output of the most recent ICAAP refresh.

Climate-related credit concentration is not a separate standing agenda item;
it is included within (ii) on a qualitative basis at the discretion of the CRO.

## 4. Reporting to the Board
The Chairperson of the RMC shall report to the Board after each meeting.
A consolidated risk dashboard shall be presented to the full Board at least
once every six months.
