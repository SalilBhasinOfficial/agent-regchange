# POL-DISC-003 — Pillar 3 Disclosure Policy
*Version 2.0. Effective 2024-10-01. Owner: Investor Relations / Finance. Approving Authority: Board of Directors.*

## 1. Purpose
This policy implements the bank's Pillar 3 disclosure obligations under the
RBI CAR Directions and the corresponding Basel III framework.

## 2. Scope of Disclosure
The bank's Pillar 3 disclosures shall cover:
- Capital structure and adequacy (CET1, AT1, Tier 2, RWAs by risk type).
- Credit risk exposures, including by geography, industry and residual maturity.
- Market risk, operational risk and equity exposures in the banking book.
- Capital Conservation Buffer maintained and any breach.
- Remuneration of senior management.

## 3. Frequency
Quantitative disclosures shall be published semi-annually on the bank's website,
and annually in the Annual Report. Qualitative disclosures shall be reviewed
annually.

## 4. Review
This policy shall be reviewed by the Board of Directors at least once a year.
