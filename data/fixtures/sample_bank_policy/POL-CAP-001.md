# POL-CAP-001 — Capital Adequacy & Buffer Maintenance Policy
*Version 4.2. Effective 2025-04-01. Owner: CFO. Approving Authority: Board of Directors.*

## 1. Purpose
This policy governs the maintenance of regulatory capital ratios and buffers
in accordance with the Reserve Bank of India (Commercial Banks — Prudential
Norms on Capital Adequacy) Directions, 2025 ("RBI CAR Directions").

## 2. Minimum Capital Ratios
The bank shall maintain, at all times, the following minimum capital ratios:
- Minimum Total Capital (MTC): 9.0% of RWAs.
- Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1): 5.5% of RWAs.
- Tier 1 Capital: 7.0% of RWAs.
- Total Capital (Tier 1 + Tier 2): 9.0% of RWAs.

## 3. Capital Conservation Buffer
Over and above the minimum CET1 of 5.5%, the bank shall maintain a Capital
Conservation Buffer (CCB) of 2.5% of RWAs in the form of CET1 capital.

## 4. Internal Capital Targets (Management Buffers)
The Asset Liability Committee shall set internal capital targets at 75 bps
above each regulatory minimum, reviewed annually by the Board.

## 5. Monitoring & Reporting
The CFO shall report capital ratios to the Risk Management Committee monthly
and to the Board quarterly. Any breach or projected breach within 60 days
shall be escalated to the Board immediately.

## 6. Review
This policy shall be reviewed by the Board of Directors at least once a year.
