# POL-ICAAP-002 — Internal Capital Adequacy Assessment Process (ICAAP) Policy
*Version 3.1. Effective 2025-07-01. Owner: CRO. Approving Authority: Board of Directors.*

## 1. Scope
The bank's Internal Capital Adequacy Assessment Process ("ICAAP") covers
Pillar 2 risks not explicitly captured under Pillar 1, including but not
limited to concentration risk, interest rate risk in the banking book,
strategic risk and reputational risk.

## 2. Governance
The Risk Management Committee of the Board ("RMC") shall oversee the ICAAP.
The CRO shall own day-to-day execution.

## 3. Cadence
The ICAAP document shall be refreshed and approved by the Board annually,
within 90 days of the financial year-end. The CRO may, at the CRO's
discretion, refresh the ICAAP intra-year following material changes in the
risk profile, but no fixed intra-year cadence is prescribed.

## 4. Stress Testing
Stress tests shall cover, at minimum, credit, market, operational and
liquidity risks. Climate-related risk is not yet a separately quantified
category in the ICAAP stress book; it is captured qualitatively in the
narrative section.

## 5. Review of Policies, Procedures and Limits
The Board shall assess and document, at least once a year, whether the
policies, procedures and limits remain appropriate.
